02 — Heritage

Built for a Maharaja, Not for Guests

Commissioned as a royal guesthouse for the Bikaner royal family and raised in warm red sandstone by master craftsmen, Laxmi Niwas Palace has watched over the desert city for more than a century.

Its carved jharokhas and domed pavilions once welcomed maharajas and viceroys. Today the same halls welcome travellers seeking real royal history, not borrowed grandeur.
